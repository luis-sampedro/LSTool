LS Current App

Mobile tool to estimate tidal current from your GPS position. The app records your position now and again 1 minute later, computes the current vector (speed & direction) and plots all measurements on a simple nautical-style chart.

Android only · Side-load installation (enable “Unknown sources” on your device).
